Local structure of smooth p-adic analytic Artin stacks
arXiv: 2603.00733 · math.AT, math.AG

Resolved Clausen’s Conjecture 5.17 by proving a local light–profinite structure for smooth p-adic analytic Artin stacks, via a van Dantzig-type theorem for groupoids and a closed-skeleton reduction. In particular, smooth p-adic analytic Artin stacks are !-good.

Derived Stone Embedding
arXiv: 2410.24077 · math.AT, math.CT

Uses ∞-topoi to partially characterize the essential image of the derived Stone embedding of the pro-category of π-finite spaces into pyknotic spaces, extending the classical Stone embedding to a derived setting.
