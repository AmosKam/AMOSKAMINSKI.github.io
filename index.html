I am a PhD student at the Weizmann Institute of Science under the supervision of Shachar Carmeli. My areas of interest are algebraic topology, algebraic geometry, and condensed mathematics.

Papers

Local structure of smooth p-adic analytic Artin stacks — Resolved Clausen's Conjecture 5.17 by proving that smooth p-adic analytic Artin stacks have a local light–profinite structure through generalizations of van Dantzig's theorem and by constructing closed skeletons. This result shows that such stacks are !-good.
arXiv:2603.00733
Derived Stone Embedding — Extended the classical characterization of the Stone embedding using ∞topoi to partially characterize the derived image of the pro-category of π-finite spaces in pyknotic spaces, thereby extending classical descriptions of profinite spaces to the derived setting.
arXiv:2410.24077
